About me

I'm Rishikesh and I'm a

I am currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) in Maharashtra (City-Kolhapur) and working as a freelance iPhone videographer and editor. My journey into content creation began in 2019 when I was an active Instagram user. As I explored the platform more deeply, I launched a new account to showcase my mobile photography and videography skills. Like most creators, I faced challenges in the beginning, with low reach and engagement. But I stayed consistent, and after a few months, one of my reels reached 8,000 views — a breakthrough that fueled my passion even more. As my academic workload increased, I found it difficult to actively shoot content. Instead of stepping away entirely, I pivoted my Instagram into a feature account that promotes other creators. This shift allowed me to balance my studies while continuing to grow the page. The account quickly gained momentum, and I started collaborating with similar pages and creators across the globe. Soon, I began receiving paid promotions and partnership offers. Today, as a freelancer, I create high-quality video content using the iPhone for a variety of clients — including political personalities, educational institutions, influencers, and fellow creators. My work reflects a strong commitment to storytelling, creativity, and visual quality. Every project I take on is a step forward in my journey as a modern content creator.
